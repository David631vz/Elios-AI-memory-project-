## Overview

**Project Sunrise** refers to a set of proposals and conceptual plans circulating in U.S. and international policy circles that outline the redevelopment of Gaza into a high-tech, infrastructure-focused urban region following large-scale destruction.

The project is **not a signed treaty**, **not a finalized construction plan**, and **not an officially adopted government program**. It exists as a **policy and investment concept**, promoted through political, advisory, and private-sector channels.

## Nature of the Proposal

Project Sunrise emphasizes:
- High-density urban redevelopment
- Technology-driven infrastructure
- Private investment and international funding
- Long-term economic integration

Critics note that:
- The proposal is discussed alongside mass displacement
- There is limited clarity on governance, consent, or resident participation
- Ethical and legal concerns remain unresolved
